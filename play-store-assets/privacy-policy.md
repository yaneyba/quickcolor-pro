# Privacy Policy for QuickColor Pro

**Last Updated:** December 29, 2025

## Introduction

QuickColor Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**QuickColor Pro does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

### What We Don't Collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Photos or images from your device

### Local Data Storage:
The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Saved color palettes
- Recent color history
- App settings and preferences
- Generated gradients

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Permissions

QuickColor Pro requires the following permissions:

### Photo Library Access (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)
**Purpose:** To allow you to select photos for color picking
**Usage:** Only accessed when you explicitly choose a photo. Photos are processed locally and never uploaded.

### Storage Access (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
**Purpose:** To save exported palettes and gradients to your device
**Usage:** Only used when you export files. No data is transmitted.

### Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
**Purpose:** To show color capture notifications
**Usage:** Local notifications only, no data sent to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

### Free Version - Advertising
The free version of QuickColor Pro displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad personalization and analytics. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [AdMob Data Usage](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings or upgrade to Pro to remove all ads.

### Pro Version - In-App Purchases
Pro upgrade purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information. Google Play's privacy policy applies:
- [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Security

All data processing happens locally on your device. We implement the following security measures:
- No server-side data storage
- No network transmission of user data
- Secure local storage using Android's built-in encryption
- No third-party analytics or tracking

## Children's Privacy

QuickColor Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is rated for Everyone and is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Showing an in-app notification (for major changes)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app
- Disable permissions in your device settings
- Opt-out of personalized ads
- Request information about data practices

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

**Email:** support@quickcolor.pro
**Response Time:** Within 24 hours

## Compliance

QuickColor Pro complies with:
- Google Play Developer Program Policies
- Android Permissions Best Practices
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

## Summary

**In short:** QuickColor Pro is a privacy-first app. We don't collect your data, we don't track you, and we don't share anything with third parties (except AdMob for ads in the free version). All your colors, palettes, and settings stay on your device.
